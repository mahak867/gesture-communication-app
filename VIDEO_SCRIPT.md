# GestureTalk — 3-Minute Demo Video Script

**Goal:** Show the problem, show the solution working with Gemma 4, leave judges moved.

---

## SHOT 1 — The Problem (0–20s)

**Visual:** Hospital ward. Patient in bed. Cannot speak.
**No voiceover needed — just show:**
- Patient trying to communicate with a nurse
- Nurse unable to understand
- Patient pointing, frustrated

**Text on screen:**
> "26 million Indians cannot speak."
> "Traditional AAC devices cost ₹80,000."
> "Most hospitals in India have none."

---

## SHOT 2 — Open the app (20–35s)

**Visual:** Phone on the hospital bed. Open GestureTalk.
**Voiceover (or text on screen):**
> "GestureTalk — powered by Gemma 4. Free forever."

Show:
- App loads
- Gemma 4 status badge in header showing "gemma4 — online"
- Camera activating

---

## SHOT 3 — Gesture detection + Gemma 4 pipeline (35–75s)

**Visual:** Patient holds fist to chest. Show the screen.

Show the **Model Pipeline tab** animating in real time:
- Stage 1: "MediaPipe — pain detected (10ms)"
- Stage 2: "Gemma 4 Vision — fist pressed to chest, distressed expression (312ms)"
- Stage 3: "Ensemble — confidence 96%"
- Stage 4: "Gemma 4 Text — 'I am in severe pain — level 8'"
- Stage 5: "Gemma 4 Translate — मुझे बहुत तेज़ दर्द हो रहा है"

**Voiceover:**
> "Gemma 4 sees the camera frame. Identifies the gesture. Completes the sentence. Translates to Hindi. In under one second."

---

## SHOT 4 — Emergency SOS (75–95s)

**Visual:** Patient uses rock-on gesture. Emergency alert fires.
Show:
- Full-screen red alarm
- Caregiver dashboard on a second device receiving the alert
- Emergency message flagged in Hindi

**Text on screen:**
> "One gesture. Nurse alerted. Patient heard."

---

## SHOT 5 — Symbol grid + SOAP note (95–130s)

**Visual:** Patient navigates symbol grid — taps Medical → pain scale.
Show:
- Caregiver dashboard: conversation log, emotion badge
- SOAP note being generated by Gemma 4:
  > "S: Patient reports severe chest pain, level 8/10..."
  > "A: Pain escalation, possible cardiac event..."

**Voiceover:**
> "Gemma 4 generates clinical notes for the doctor. From a gesture."

---

## SHOT 6 — The mission (130–180s)

**Visual:** Montage of the features — symbol grid, fatigue mode, ISL phrases, Hindi translation, caregiver dashboard.

**Text on screen (one line at a time):**
> "Full A–Z gestures. Symbol grid. Pain scale. Emergency SOS."
> "Hindi. Tamil. Telugu. Bengali. Marathi. Punjabi."
> "ISL support. FHIR export. Voice banking for ALS patients."
> "Works offline. On any phone."

Final card:
> "GestureTalk"
> "₹0. Free forever. For everyone."
> "github.com/mahak867/gesture-communication-app"
> "gesturetalk.vercel.app"

---

## Recording tips

- Film on your laptop showing the actual app running with Ollama
- Make sure the Gemma 4 status badge shows "online" — proves it's real
- Show the pipeline tab with real latency numbers — this is what judges need to see
- The Hindi translation appearing is the emotional peak — linger on it for 3 seconds
- Don't narrate everything — let the UI speak
- Total runtime: under 3 minutes (hackathon requirement)
